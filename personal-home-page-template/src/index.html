My Bio



I have spent the last 15 years working in customer service, sales and complaint handling roles. I am now looking for a change of career and have recently developed an interest in learning to code.
I learned all about the great things I could achieve through Code Your Future from someone who had successfully completed the course and is now loving his new career in coding.
Whilst I've mostly enjoyed the jobs that I've had, none of them really felt like a career to me. To put it simply; I want to feel like I'm achieving more than I have done so far.
I'm excited by coding because the possibilities are endless and it can help me to develop the meaningful career that I have been looking for.

My Future

By this time next year, I hope to have completed a nine month course with Code Your Future and have started my first full-time employed role in coding.
Looking further down the line, in five years time, I hope to have developed the skills needed to work for myself.
In doing this, I'll be able to live a more free life and take my work in coding with me, wherever I choose to visit around the world!
